Komatsu D375 — Water Pump Failure RCA

Problem

Repeated driven-gear damage leading to contamination risk and unplanned downtime.

Analysis

RCA (5‑Whys), teardown with OEM; checked clearances, wear patterns, and contamination routes.

Action

Updated service intervals, parts quality checks, contamination barriers, and operator checks.

Outcome

Repeat incidents reduced; improved engine protection and availability.
